Our Heritage

A Legacy of Real Estate Excellence

Founded in 2009 with a singular vision: to create world-class real estate investments that deliver exceptional value to investors while contributing meaningfully to the communities we serve.

What began as a focused US-based operation has grown into a truly international enterprise with active development portfolios spanning three continents. Our Egypt branch manages landmark projects across Upper Egypt, while our Turkey operations deliver premium residential and commercial properties in Istanbul and key cities.

DIYAR Estates brings together the best of American corporate governance, Middle Eastern market insight, and global investment acumen — creating a unique competitive advantage in every market we enter.
