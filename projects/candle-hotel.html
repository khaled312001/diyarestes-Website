Candle Hotel Dendera

Dendera, Qena Governorate, Upper Egypt

Project Overview

Where Ancient Egypt Meets Modern Luxury

The Candle Hotel – Dendera is DIYAR Estates' flagship hospitality development, strategically located near the legendary Dendera Temple Complex in Qena Governorate, Upper Egypt. This premium 5-star hotel blends contemporary architectural elegance with authentic Egyptian heritage motifs.

Designed to serve the growing number of cultural tourism visitors to one of Egypt's most spectacular ancient sites, the hotel features 180 premium rooms and suites, world-class dining, a rooftop pool with panoramic views, conference facilities, and a luxury spa inspired by ancient Egyptian wellness traditions.

The Candle Hotel represents a transformative investment for Upper Egypt's hospitality sector, expected to generate significant returns while elevating the region's international tourism appeal.
