Project Overview

American Luxury Living Redefined

Diyar Luxury Residences is DIYAR Estates' flagship US residential development — an exclusive gated community designed to redefine premium living in one of America's most desirable metropolitan markets.

The development features a curated collection of custom-designed single-family homes and premium townhouses, each built to the highest American construction standards with European finishing influences. Smart home technology, private landscaped gardens, resort-style amenities, and a secure gated environment create an unparalleled living experience.

From an investment standpoint, the project offers strong capital appreciation potential in a high-demand, supply-constrained market, alongside attractive rental yield opportunities in the premium residential segment.
