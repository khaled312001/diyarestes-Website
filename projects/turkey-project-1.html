Bosphorus View Residences

Istanbul, Turkey

Project Overview

Where the Bosphorus Meets Architectural Luxury

Bosphorus View Residences represents DIYAR Estates' premier Istanbul development — a collection of 120 luxury apartments and penthouse suites positioned to offer spectacular, unobstructed views of the Bosphorus Strait in one of the world's most iconic cities.

Each residence is finished to the highest international standards, featuring premium Italian marble, smart home automation systems, floor-to-ceiling glazing to maximize the iconic Istanbul views, and private balconies designed as extensions of the living space. Building amenities include a rooftop infinity pool overlooking the Bosphorus, fitness center, concierge services, and a residents-only social lounge.

The development qualifies for Turkey's Citizenship by Investment program, offering investors a clear pathway to Turkish citizenship upon qualifying purchase, in addition to excellent capital appreciation and rental yield potential in Istanbul's premium residential market.
