Project Overview

Productive Land Investment in Upper Egypt

The 160 Feddan Farm represents one of DIYAR Estates' most productive and immediately income-generating agricultural investments. Fully operational and generating returns, this managed agricultural estate in Upper Egypt delivers consistent yields through a diversified crop strategy.

The farm utilizes proven modern agricultural practices including precision irrigation, soil optimization, and integrated pest management. Its proximity to major transport links ensures efficient logistics for domestic and export markets. The farm is fully managed by DIYAR Estates' expert agricultural team, providing a truly passive investment experience for investors.

With full title deeds, clear legal ownership structures, and documented production history, the 160 Feddan Farm offers one of the lowest-risk, highest-transparency agricultural investment opportunities in Egypt.
